{% blocktrans %}About Us{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}We offer the best tour packages to fit your budget.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} SILK ROUTE DMC (Destination Management Company) welcomes you to an extraordinary journey through the East, delving deep into traditions and histories spanning thousands of years. We are committed to providing you with an unforgettable holiday, showcasing the jewels of Central Asia, such as Samarkand, Bukhara, and Khiva. ... Our professional team guarantees high-quality service and aims to enhance your understanding of our culture, history, and geography. We offer a wide range of services, including:
- Guided tours to the cities of the Great Silk Road: Tashkent, Samarkand, Bukhara, Khiva, and other historical sites.
- Visa support.
- Hotel booking with various levels of comfort.
- Rental services for vehicles of different classes.
- Booking of air and train tickets.

- Arrangements for children's and student groups.

- Participation in Uzbekistan's various festivals.

- Tailored tours encompassing bike trips, photo tours, eco-tours, and beyond.
Our guides are highly qualified, dependable, and possess essential personal qualities. They are proficient in various foreign languages, including Turkish, Russian, English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Japanese, and more. The excursions designed by our specialists are individualized, incorporating fascinating historical facts without adhering to standard templates. Our guides and translators boast deep knowledge of the region's history, as well as the culture and traditions of the local populace. They will regale you with legends and stories, immersing you in the enchanting ambiance of the East. Join us and immerse yourself in the unique beauty of Uzbekistan with SILKROUTE! {% endblocktrans %}
